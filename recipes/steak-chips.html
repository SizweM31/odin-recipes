Pan-fried Steak and Chips

Juicy Steak and Chips combo

Steak and Chips Recipe

Ingredients

  • Potatoes, cut into wedges
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Mint leaves
  • Lemon, squeezed
  • peas
  • Beef sirloin steak

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to gas 5, 190°C, fan 170°C. In a roasting tray, toss the potatoes with half the oil, the paprika, and a little seasoning. Roast in the oven for 35 minutes, until golden.
  2. Brush the steaks with a little of the remaining oil and set aside. Whisk the rest of the oil with the lemon juice and seasoning, then drizzle over the lettuce and peas.
  3. Heat a frying pan until hot, add the steak and fry for 4-5 minutes on each side, until cooked through. Transfer to a board for 5 minutes to rest.
  4. Pour the pan juices over the peas. Slice the steak and serve with the potato wedges and vegetables.
